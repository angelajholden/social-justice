Global Citizenship

Historically Awakening Seed has been conscious of life on Planet Earth. From the early days of writing letters to save the whales, to fundraisers for world hunger and to support the Red Cross after 9/11, we've let children know about global events and set up activities that helped them feel like they could be part of the solution.
